CS 350S: Privacy-Preserving Systems

Resources

The following textbooks are good resources for cryptography:
  • A Graduate Course in Applied Cryptography (Boneh-Shoup)
  • Introduction to Modern Cryptography (Katz-Lindell)
You can also look at the proceedings of security conferences, such as IEEE Security and Privacy and USENIX Security, as well as systems conferences, such as SOSP and OSDI.
